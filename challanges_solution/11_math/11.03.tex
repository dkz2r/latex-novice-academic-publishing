\section{Referencing Equations}
		
	We can include a numbered equation as follows:
	
	\begin{equation}
		E = mc^2
		\label{eq:energy}
	\end{equation}
	
	Now, we refer to the equation in the text. The famous equation is \ref{eq:energy}.
